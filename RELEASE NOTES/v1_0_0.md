# King Jump v1.0 – Initial Release

King Jump is a 2D medieval platformer and literacy game developed in Godot. Players take on the role of an exiled king attemtping to regain control of his castle. Fight your way through different levels, answer literacy questions, and defeat the necromancer to reclaim the castle.

---

## Features

### Game systems
- Various enemy Ai designed with different difficulties:
  - Easy Skeleton
  - Hard Skeleton
  - Golem
  - Gollux (Mini-Boss)
  - Necromancer (Final Boss)
- Sword, spear, and shield combat.
- Healing system.
- Inventory systems.

### Literacy Gameplay
- Literacy questions are integrated into gameplay.
- Correct answers reward the player.
- Incorrect answers add **time penalties**, encouraging both speed and accuracy.
    - _NOTE: Time spent on questions does not contribute to the overall time._

### Exploration
- Five handcrafted dungeon levels with unique layouts and lighting.
- Interactive minimap showing:
  - Player location
  - Enemies
  - Collectibles
  - Important objectives

### Visuals & Audio
- Original pixel-art environments.
- Animated enemies and bosses.
- Sound effects and background music throughout the game.

## Controls

| Action | Control |
|--------|---------|
| Move | A / D |
| Jump | Space / W |
| Sprint | Shift / F |
| Attack | Left Click |
| Block | Right Click (Shield Equipped) |
| Throw Spear | Left Click (Spear Equipped) |
| Drop Item | Q |
| Interact | E |
